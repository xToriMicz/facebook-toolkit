Affiliate Post Examples

Example 1: Storytelling Approach

3 months ago my back pain was so bad I couldn't sleep. Tried everything — expensive mattresses, chiropractor, yoga. Nothing worked long-term. Then a friend recommended this ergonomic pillow. I was skeptical (another gimmick?) but tried it anyway. Week 1: Slightly better Week 2: Noticeably less pain Week 3: First full night sleep in months Now 3 months later — zero back pain. Not exaggerating. I'm not saying it'll work for everyone. But for 890 baht, it's worth trying. DM me "pillow" for the link. #backpain #sleepbetter #honestshare
Why:Personal story + timeline + honest disclaimer. "DM me" creates private conversation = higher conversion than public link.

Example 2: Comparison Post

Tested 3 wireless earbuds under 1,000 baht. Brand A (499): Sound 6/10, Battery 4hrs, Build 5/10 Brand B (799): Sound 8/10, Battery 6hrs, Build 7/10 Brand C (990): Sound 9/10, Battery 8hrs, Build 9/10 Winner: Brand B — best value. Brand C is better but not 2x better for the price. Full comparison with photos in comments! #earbuds #techreview #comparison
Why:Comparison = high save rate. People bookmark these. "Full comparison in comments" drives comment engagement.

Example 3: Problem-Solution (Short)

Kitchen grease won't come off? This 89 baht spray cleaned my 3-year-old grease stain in 30 seconds. Before/after photo attached. Type "clean" in comments for the link. #kitchenhack #cleaningtip
Why:Short + visual proof. Before/after photos are the highest-engagement format on Facebook.

Caption Hook Formulas

Hooks that stop the scroll:

  • "I made a mistake buying [X]. Here's why..."
  • "[Number] things I wish I knew before..."
  • "This [cheap/free thing] changed my [routine]"
  • "Before you buy [X], read this"
  • "Honest review: [product] after [time period]"

CTA (Call-to-Action) That Work

  • High conversion: "DM me [keyword]" — creates private conversation
  • High engagement: "Comment [word] for the link" — boosts algorithm
  • Medium: "Link in first comment" — auto-comment handles this
  • Avoid: Direct link in caption — Facebook suppresses reach 40-60%
Important:Facebook algorithm rewards posts that generate comments in the first 30 minutes. Schedule posts when your audience is online and reply to every comment quickly.

How to set up:1. Write all 7 posts as Drafts. 2. Go to Schedule/Bulk. 3. Select all 7 drafts. 4. Set start time = Monday 12:00, interval = 24hr. 5. Done!

Content Mix Rule

80/20 Rule:

  • 80% value — tips, stories, education, entertainment
  • 20% sell — product reviews, affiliate, promotions

In our 7-day plan: 5 value posts (Mon/Tue/Thu/Sat/Sun) + 2 sell posts (Wed/Fri) = 71% value. Perfect.

Pro tip:Use AI Writer to generate all 7 posts at once. Set different tones: Mon=Educational, Tue=Fun, Wed=Sales, etc. Edit each one to add your voice, then save as drafts.
