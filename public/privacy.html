Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Facebook Toolkit ("we", "our", "the app") at fb.makeloops.xyz is operated by xToriMicz. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you log in with Facebook, we receive:

  • Public profile — your name and profile picture
  • Email address — for account identification
  • Page Access Token — to post content on your behalf to Facebook Pages you manage
  • Page information — Page ID and Page name for Pages you grant access to

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the app
  • To post text and images to your Facebook Page on your behalf
  • To store your post history within the app
  • To upload and temporarily store images you choose to post

3. Data Storage

  • Your data is stored on Cloudflare infrastructure (Workers KV, D1 database, R2 storage)
  • Access tokens are stored securely in Cloudflare KV
  • Images you upload are stored in Cloudflare R2
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties

4. Facebook Data

  • We only access Facebook data that you explicitly authorize
  • We use the pages_manage_posts permission solely to publish content you create
  • We do not read or store your personal Facebook messages, friends list, or timeline posts
  • We do not use your data for advertising or tracking

5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by:

  • Removing the app from your Facebook Business Integrations settings
  • Contacting us at the email below

Upon deletion request, we will remove all your data including tokens, post history, and uploaded images within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as you use the app
  • Post history is retained for your reference
  • If you delete your account, all data is removed within 30 days

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies only for authentication (session management). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Security

We protect your data using:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure token storage in Cloudflare KV (not in browser localStorage)
  • No client-side exposure of access tokens

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion:
